About FTMA - Football Team of the Chadian Community in Saudi Arabia

Welcome to FTMA, the heart of Chadian football in Saudi Arabia. Established in 2015, our mission is to unite the Chadian community through the love of football and to celebrate our cultural heritage while promoting sportsmanship and teamwork.

  • Champion of 2024: We proudly held the championship title in 2024, showcasing our dedication and skill on the field.
  • Community Engagement: Our activities extend beyond the pitch, involving various outreach programs and events that strengthen the bonds within our community.
  • Talent Development: We are committed to nurturing young talents through our youth programs, providing them with opportunities to excel in football and life.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is COVID-19?

    COVID-19 is a respiratory illness caused by the coronavirus SARS-CoV-2. It spreads primarily through respiratory droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or talks.

  • What are the symptoms of COVID-19?

    Common symptoms include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. Other symptoms may include fatigue, muscle or body aches, loss of taste or smell, sore throat, congestion, nausea, or diarrhea.

  • How can I prevent myself from COVID-19?

    To prevent COVID-19, practice good hygiene by washing hands frequently, wearing masks in public settings, maintaining physical distance, and avoiding close contact with sick individuals.

  • What should I do if I feel sick?

    If you feel sick with COVID-19 symptoms, stay home, isolate yourself from others, monitor your symptoms, and seek medical attention if symptoms worsen or you have difficulty breathing.

  • How can I care for a loved one who is sick with COVID-19?

    If caring for a loved one with COVID-19, provide supportive care, monitor their symptoms, encourage rest and hydration, and seek medical advice if symptoms worsen.
